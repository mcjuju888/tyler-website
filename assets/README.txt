Drop your media files in this folder:

  hero.mp4         - background video for the home page hero
                     (any color video works - the site renders it black & white)
  hero-poster.jpg  - optional still image shown while the video loads
  about.jpg        - your photo for the About Me section (portrait, ~4:5 ratio)

Until these exist, the site shows a dark gradient hero and a striped
photo placeholder, so nothing looks broken.
